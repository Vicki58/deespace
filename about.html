Our story

We build the digital
infrastructure that makes
businesses visible.

Deespace was founded in Nairobi by three specialists who saw the same problem from three different angles: too many great businesses across Africa remain invisible — not because they lack quality, but because they lack the digital infrastructure to be seen.

We came together — an AI engineer, a full-stack developer, and a creative director — because we believed no single discipline could solve this alone. A great website needs great engineering. Great engineering needs a brand worth building around. And a brand worth building around needs intelligent systems behind it.

That's Deespace: three specialists, one unified team, building everything a business needs to compete — websites, apps, brand identity, marketing, and AI-powered tools — without the gaps that come from working with five different freelancers.
