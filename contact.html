Frequently asked questions

How long does a typical project take?
It depends on scope — a simple website typically takes 2–4 weeks, while full branding and web projects can take 4–8 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline after our first conversation.
Do you work with businesses outside Kenya?
Yes. While we're based in Nairobi and focused on Africa's growth, we work with clients anywhere — everything is delivered remotely with clear communication.
Can you handle a project that needs multiple services?
Absolutely — that's exactly what Deespace was built for. Our team covers web development, design, marketing, and AI/tech under one roof, so multi-service projects are handled by one cohesive team.
What if I'm not sure what I need yet?
That's completely fine. Reach out anyway — our discovery process is designed to help you clarify your goals before we recommend a solution.
